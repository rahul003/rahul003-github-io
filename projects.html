2 Pass Assembler Linker Loader for 8085

April 2013

GuideDr. Santosh Biswas, Assistant Professor, Dept. of Computer Science and Engineering, IIT Guwahati
NatureCurriculum Project
DetailsDeveloped a 2 pass 8085 assembler, linker and loader in Python.
